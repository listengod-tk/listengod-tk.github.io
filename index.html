    Children are one of the largest new user groups of mobile technology. The need has never been greater to understand how these technologies are being designed and to evaluate their impact on children. The technology for children is good in terms of education and learning which improves their visualization.

    However, what about the imagination that grows only when we read and listen. That's why we have created an audio bible story for them to decorate their imagination.

Gospel Audio Books

My heart is stirred by a noble theme as I recite my verses for the king; my tongue is the pen of a skillful writer. - Psalm 45:1

As the verse describes, there are blessed writers whom God has chosen to express himself as words in and through them. The words might have reached many readers and changed their lives. But, the words that are written by them is stopped with an only limited audience of people having reading habits.

What about soul that doesn't like to read or don't know to read?

We Believe, God words that have written by them should reach to all people in the world.

That's why ListenGod team decided to use our technical capabilities to convert the paper books as audio books or podcast using software such as TTS (text to speech), artificial intelligence, deep learning and voice assistance to deliver the Godly content to a wider audience.

So! signup to our ListenGod platform and experience word of God from different perspective.

Gospel Songs

He put a new song in my mouth, a hymn of praise to our God.

There are plenty of platforms available out there and those are filled with hymn of praise to our God by artiest whose mouth filled with songs by our Lord.

It is good! , but we felt that the gospel songs that are available in common platform may distract the listener as it is written in the scripture Matthew 13:7 "Other seed fell among thorns, which grew up and choked the plants. -".

Hence, ListenGod team decided to build the static Website that curl across the YouTube channels and bring you the quality HD Gospel songs in MP3 format without scarifying the YouTube copyright by following this simple techniques.
